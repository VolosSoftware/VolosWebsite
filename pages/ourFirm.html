About Us

         Volos Portfolio Solutions, LLC provides optimization software for financial derivative strategies. Active managers have trade ideas that are more intricate than those of regular investors. We believe that those intricate ideas are not expressed efficiently in financial markets through simple long/short positions. The Volos platform allows managers to translate their ideas into derivative strategies that are optimally suited for expressing their detailed views.

         After finding optimal derivative trades through stochastic optimization and machine learning, the Volos platform provides managers with advanced visual and statistical tools for detailed analysis. The Volos platform offers managers an industry-wide edge by allowing them to express their ideas as effectively as possible.
